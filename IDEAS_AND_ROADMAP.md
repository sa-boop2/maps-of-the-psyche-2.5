# PSYCHE 2.0 — Expansion Ideas & Roadmap

This document outlines potential future modules, views, and frameworks for the **Maps of the Psyche** application, focusing on practically applying philosophical and psychological information.

---

## 1. The "Dream Alchemist" (Oneiric Mapping)
**Framework**: Jungian Dream Analysis + Senoi Dream Work.

- **The View**: A "Night Mode" ethereal interface where users log their dreams as "Substances" (e.g., Alchemical Lead).
- **Practical Application**: An AI or manual tagger extracts core symbols (e.g., *The Dark Water, The Lost Child, The Tower*).
- **Visual Impact**: The 3D Psyche Map highlights the corresponding archetype nodes, showing which "territories" of the unconscious are currently active in the user's life.

## 2. The "Biological Mirror" (Neuropsychology Overlay)
**Framework**: Affective Neuroscience (Jaak Panksepp) + Neuro-Psychoanalysis (Mark Solms).

- **The View**: A toggleable anatomical overlay for the 3D Sphere.
- **Information-Based**: Maps abstract concepts (Ego, Shadow, Collective Unconscious) onto brain systems (Prefrontal Cortex, Amygdala, Periaqueductal Gray).
- **Practicality**: Provides a "hard science" grounding. When examining "Eternal Return" in a spiritual context, the user can toggle the "Biological Mirror" to see the corresponding dopamine/SEEKING circuits involved in obsessive patterns.

## 3. The "Labyrinth of Ancestors" (Systemic Mapping)
**Framework**: Internal Family Systems (IFS) + Family Constellations.

- **The View**: An interactive "Constellation" view where "Internal Parts" are mapped as satellites orbiting the central Self node.
- **Practicality**: Users can map out their "Inner Family" (The Manager, The Firefighter, The Exile).
- **Visual Impact**: Visualizes internal resistance or harmony. If a "Protector" part is dominant, it might physically obscure access to a "Shadow" node on the 3D map until it is "unburdened."

## 4. "The Hero’s Path" (Chronos View)
**Framework**: Narrative Therapy + Joseph Campbell’s Monomyth.

- **The View**: A temporal spiral winding through the 3D psyche space.
- **Practicality**: Users plot their personal biography onto the map. Major life events (The Call to Adventure, The Ordeal, The Return) are pinned as milestones.
- **Information-Based**: Correlates personal life stages with the "Ages of Man" across different cultural traditions.

## 5. "The Alchemical Lab" (Integration Workflows)
**Framework**: Hermetic Alchemy as Psychological Transformation.

- **The View**: A dashboard styled after a 17th-century laboratory.
- **Practicality**: A structured workflow for integrating Shadow Scanner results.
    - **Nigredo (Blackening)**: Confronting the shadow data.
    - **Albedo (Whitening)**: Finding the "gift" or repressed potential.
    - **Rubedo (Reddening)**: Embodying the integrated trait in daily life.
- **Gamification**: Provides a sense of progression from "Lead" (unconscious habit) to "Gold" (integrated awareness).
